Passions & Interests

Hobbies are what keep life interesting. Whether it's listening to music, gaming, or diving into a good book, I love exploring new ways to unwind and be creative. Here, you’ll find what I enjoy most in my free time

Music

Music has the power to uplift and energize. Whether you're listening to your favorite playlist, exploring new genres, or attending live performances, music offers an emotional connection that can be deeply personal.

Game

I love gaming, especially those that let me dive into fantasy worlds or challenge my strategic thinking. Whether it’s playing a competitive multiplayer game or a single-player RPG, gaming is my way of unwinding and having fun with friends.

Reading

Reading has been one of my favorite pastimes for as long as I can remember. From gripping novels to interesting short stories, I love escaping into different worlds through books. Whether it’s fiction or non-fiction, there’s always something new to discover.

Life in Motion: My Everyday Activities

I love spending weekends exploring new places and trying new things. My daily routines keep me active and make life exciting.

Daily Routines

My daily routine plays a big role in shaping my day. I usually start with a cup of coffee to wake up, followed by a bit of exercise to energize myself for the day ahead. Then, I dive into work or school tasks, staying productive with a clear plan in mind.

Helping Out and Gaining Experience

Every week, I step in to help out by selling goods at my neighbor’s house in place of my mom. It’s a routine I’ve come to enjoy as it not only allows me to lend a hand but also gives me valuable experience in managing sales, interacting with customers, and keeping track of inventory.
